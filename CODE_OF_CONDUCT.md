# Code of conduct

Arithmon is a research program. Discussion here is expected to be scientific,
specific, and civil.

Expected:

- Argue about claims, methods, and evidence, not about people.
- Be precise. Point to the line, the file, the number, or the reference.
- Assume good faith, and correct the record when you are shown to be wrong.

Not accepted:

- Personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language.
- Deliberate misrepresentation of results, your own or other people's.

Concerns about conduct can be raised privately with brieuc@bdelaf.com.
